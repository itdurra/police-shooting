Police Shootings by Gender

Key: Female Male

Displayed above is a map depicting police shootings arranged by gender. Blue dots represent police shootings involving male suspects while red dots represent shootings involving female suspects. The map shows a clear disparity between the genders of victims of police shootings being heavily skewed towards males.
